Higher score means HIGHER PAY

Workers scores are computed at the END OF EACH day on the following baisis:

  • For every blank that is double verfied by another worker +1
  • For every blank that is disagreement from at least two other workers -2
  • For every blank where no agreement is reached between a set of 3 workers 0

Workers are then placed into tiers with the following benefits:
Tier Score Benefits
Training Camp Less than 50 points none
Rookie Greater than 50 points none
Scholar Top 50% of workers with over 50 points Qualify for higher wage tasks
Rising Star Top 15% of workers with over 1000 points 10% higher wages
Superstar Star Top 5% of workers with atleast 1500 points 50% higher wages!
